## Claim Levels

The site uses the following evidence statuses:

- `confirmed`;
- `confirmed_from_official_source_data`;
- `derived_confirmed`;
- `candidate`;
- `candidate_digitized`;
- `missing`;
- `unavailable`.

Teaching models are correct at their stated approximation level but are not paper-level reproductions. A Methods-level reproduction requires source-locked Hamiltonian structure, coupling coefficients, Zeeman offsets, interaction tensors, pulse timing, noise channels and metric conventions.

## Maintenance Rule

Every new numeric value should include a unit, source, evidence status and applicability note. Cross-platform fidelity comparisons must normalize loss, leakage, SPAM and conditioning conventions before interpretation.
